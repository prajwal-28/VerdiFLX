## **1. Technology Stack**

- **Prospecting:** _LinkedIn Sales Navigator_ – Find companies/contacts by industry, title, location; **useful** for precisely identifying OEM/Tier-1 auto manufacturers and their procurement leads. _Apollo.io_ – Search company databases and purchase verified lists; **useful** for building a high-quality lead list based on ICP criteria.

- **Lead Enrichment:** _Clearbit (or ZoomInfo)_ – Append details (job title, email) from company domains; **useful** to verify correct decision-makers (Head of Procurement/Supply Chain) and obtain direct contact info. _Hunter.io_ – Find and verify business email addresses; **useful** for ensuring outreach emails reach valid addresses.

- **Email Outreach:** _Salesloft (or Outreach.io)_ – Manage and automate cold-email sequences; **useful** for tracking opens/clicks/replies, scheduling follow-ups, and personalizing at scale. _Mailshake (or Lemlist)_ – Lightweight email campaign tool; **useful** for sending targeted templates and A/B testing subject lines.

- **LinkedIn Outreach:** _LinkedIn Sales Navigator_ – (also used for outreach) enables direct InMail and connection requests to procurement leads; **useful** as procurement professionals are active on LinkedIn. _Dux-Soup (or Zopto)_ – Automate LinkedIn connection requests and messaging; **useful** to scale LinkedIn outreach (e.g. sending personalized invites/follow-ups).

- **CRM:** _HubSpot CRM (or Salesforce)_ – Track all prospect companies, contacts, outreach activities and deals; **useful** as a single source of truth for pipeline and follow-ups. _Pipedrive_ – Visual deal pipeline for SMB teams; **useful** if a simpler, sales-focused CRM is preferred.

- **Automation:** _Zapier (or Make/Integromat)_ – Connect apps (e.g. form fills → CRM, email replies → CRM tasks); **useful** to automate data flow (e.g. new meeting booked → calendar invite, Slack alert). _HubSpot Workflows_ – (if using HubSpot) automate email sequences or lead scoring; **useful** for automoving leads through stages (e.g. convert a meeting to “Booked”).

- **Analytics:** _Google Looker Studio (Data Studio)_ – Build custom dashboards for email/LinkedIn KPIs; **useful** to visualize the campaign funnel (open rates, replies, meetings) in real time. _Tableau (or Power BI)_ – Consolidate data from CRM and outreach tools; **useful** for high-level executive reports on campaign performance. _CRM Dashboards_ – Native reporting (e.g. HubSpot Reports); **useful** to monitor targets (emails sent, meetings booked) directly from the CRM.

## **4. Dashboard Content**

### KPI Overview

- **Companies Targeted:** _Companies in ICP list._ Why: defines pipeline scope. Target (example): **50** ; Actual: **45** . Status: **Good** (on track to reach total list).
- **Emails Sent:** Total outbound emails delivered. Why: measure outreach volume. Target: **200** ; Actual: **185** . Status: **Good** .
- **LinkedIn Requests Sent:** Connection invites sent to leads. Why: tracks social outreach effort. Target: **50** ; Actual: **50** . Status: **Good** .
- **Open Rate:** % of emails opened. Why: indicates subject-line effectiveness. Target: **≥30%** ; Example Actual: **25%** . Status: **Warning** (below target – suggests improving subject lines).
- **Reply Rate:** % of emails that got any reply. Why: shows initial engagement level. Target: **≥5%** ; Actual: **3%** . Status: **Warning** (low replies – may need better personalization).
- **Positive Reply Rate:** % of replies that are interest or meeting requests. Why: measures qualified interest. Target: **≥2%** ; Actual: **1%** . Status: **Critical** (few positive replies – refine value pitch/CTA).
- **Meetings Booked:** Number of demos/calls scheduled. Why: direct pipeline progress. Target: **3** ; Actual: **1** . Status: **Critical** (needs improvement in conversion).
