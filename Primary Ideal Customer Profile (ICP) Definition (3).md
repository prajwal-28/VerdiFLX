# Primary Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Definition

## Core ICP Definition

Our single best-fit ICP is **automotive manufacturers** (OEMs or Tier-1 suppliers) that mold plastic parts. Specifically, companies in the **Automotive** vertical producing injection-molded **polyolefin components** – e.g. dashboards, interior trim, exterior body panels, fairings and fuel tanks. These products are typically made from PP/PE, which VerdiFLX can replace. The decision-maker is the **Head of Procurement / Sourcing** (or equivalent Supply Chain Director) at the OEM or Tier-1. This role controls raw-material purchasing and can approve a switch to VerdiFLX.

VerdiFLX is a drop-in recycled PP/PE alternative (50% post-consumer recycled content) that requires **no tooling changes**. By adopting VerdiFLX, auto manufacturers can meet India's tightening recycled-content mandates (e.g. 40% recycled plastic in packaging and products by 2026) and global ELV targets while lowering carbon footprint. In short, VerdiFLX lets auto makers substitute high-quality recycled material for virgin plastic without performance loss, aligning with both regulatory pressure and their sustainability goals.
