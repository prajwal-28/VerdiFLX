Suitability Rationale

Hero uses vast PP/PE content in its bikes; switching to VerdiFLX (a drop-in recycled PP with 50% post-consumer recycled content) lets Hero meet new recycled-content regulations and bolster its green branding – all without retooling existing production. By adopting VerdiFLX, auto manufacturers can meet India's tightening recycled-content mandates (e.g. 40% recycled plastic in packaging and products by 2026) and global ELV targets while lowering carbon footprint without performance loss.

Prospecting Tools

LinkedIn Sales Navigator – Find companies/contacts by industry, title, location; useful for precisely identifying OEM/Tier-1 auto manufacturers and their procurement leads.
Apollo.io – Search company databases and purchase verified lists; useful for building a high-quality lead list based on ICP criteria.

Lead Enrichment

Clearbit (or ZoomInfo) – Append details (job title, email) from company domains; useful to verify correct decision-makers (Head of Procurement/Supply Chain) and obtain direct contact info.
Hunter.io – Find and verify business email addresses; useful for ensuring outreach emails reach valid addresses.

Email Outreach Automation

Salesloft (or Outreach.io) – Manage and automate cold-email sequences; useful for tracking opens/clicks/replies, scheduling follow-ups, and personalizing at scale.
Mailshake (or Lemlist) – Lightweight email campaign tool; useful for sending targeted templates and A/B testing subject lines.

LinkedIn Outreach Automation

LinkedIn Sales Navigator – Enables direct InMail and connection requests to procurement leads; useful as procurement professionals are active on LinkedIn.
Dux-Soup (or Zopto) – Automate LinkedIn connection requests and messaging; useful to scale LinkedIn outreach (e.g. sending personalized invites/follow-ups).

CRM Integration

HubSpot CRM (or Salesforce) – Track all prospect companies, contacts, outreach activities and deals; useful as a single source of truth for pipeline and follow-ups.
Pipedrive – Visual deal pipeline for SMB teams; useful if a simpler, sales-focused CRM is preferred.

Workflow Automation

Zapier (or Make/Integromat) – Connect apps (e.g. form fills → CRM, email replies → CRM tasks); useful to automate data flow (e.g. new meeting booked → calendar invite, Slack alert).
HubSpot Workflows – Automate email sequences or lead scoring; useful for automoving leads through stages (e.g. convert a meeting to "Booked").

Analytics & Reporting

Google Looker Studio (Data Studio) – Build custom dashboards for email/LinkedIn KPIs; useful to visualize the campaign funnel in real time.
Tableau (or Power BI) – Consolidate data from CRM and outreach tools; useful for high-level executive reports on campaign performance.
CRM Dashboards – Native reporting (e.g. HubSpot Reports); useful to monitor targets (emails sent, meetings booked) directly from the CRM.
